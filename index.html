What can you do?

Here is some little things that if we all do, will help getting things back.

Plant a tree

Create your own green space. Add pot plants to your window sill or balcony.

Cut consumption

Avoid single-use items and fast fashion, and try not to buy more than you need.

Drive less

Instead of getting in the car, walk or cycle and enjoy the physical health benefits.

Use less water

Wasting water impacts carbon footprint and air quality, and depletes your fossil fuel resources.

About

Releaf is a nonprofit, independent media organization dedicated to telling stories of climate solutions and a just future. Our goal is to use the power of storytelling to illuminate the way toward a better world, inspire millions of people to walk that path with us, and show that the time for action is now. At Releaf we engage our audience to ensure that as progress is made no one is left behind. We have until roughly 2030 to slash our carbon emissions to lessen the greatest impacts of climate change and we need to innovate and adapt to a future that is quickly becoming a reality. We're committed to the stories, the people, and the ideas that will make this possible. For all of this, GreenWave relies on you. We count on corporate and a growing community of members.
